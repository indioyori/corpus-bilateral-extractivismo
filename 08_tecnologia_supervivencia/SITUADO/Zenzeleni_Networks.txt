## Welcome to Zenzeleni Community Networks

Zenzeleni is a community owned wireless internet service provider based in rural South Africa. We offer quality, high speed internet comparable to the country’s most developed urban centres. Our internet is truly affordable. Our model aims to significantly cut costs of telecommunications, retain expenditure within communities as a form of social entrepreneurship, and support the development of a rural digital ecosystem towards bridging the digital divide.

While we are rooted in communities, we also engage actively with the wider telecommunications and digital innovation sectors in advocating for meaningful policy, the right to access, digital equity, service equality and diversity of expression.

Since 2012, we have slowly but steadily developed Zenzeleni into an internationally recognized Community Network, driven by a desire to bring the kinds of opportunities that internet connectivity and cooperative ownership has to offer to our communities – especially our children.

We have created strategic partnerships which offer support and guidance, nurturing our development and sustainable progress. This has been critical in getting Community Networks recognized as mechanisms for positive change and connectivity in South Africa, throughout Africa and internationally.

We are growing. Join us. Get connected. Zenzeleni! (Do it yourself!).

## SERVICE UPDATE - JUNE 2025

Our community was struck by devastating winds exceeding 100km/h on June 9th, which tragically claimed lives across the Eastern Cape and destroyed vital infrastructure throughout our region. Our main communications tower was completely destroyed in the storm, disrupting internet connectivity for residents in Mankosi, Zithulele, and surrounding areas.

We understand how crucial reliable internet access is for our community, especially during these challenging times. Our team is working around the clock to restore full service as quickly as possible. We're committed to reconnecting our communities and will provide updates on our progress.
